01 / thesis

the thesis.

most companies want to be the platform. we go the other way.

make the smallest useful thing. make it well. give it away.

a tool that does one job. a game that takes twenty minutes. a tutorial on one concept. a protocol that slots into someone else's stack.

the bet — small pieces compound. a hundred good fragments beat one mediocre platform.

03 / how it works

principles.

  1. 01

    ethics over profit.

    if it makes money by making the product worse or the user more exploited, it doesn't get made. non-negotiable.

  2. 02

    accessibility is a feature.

    screen reader. cheap laptop. never opened a terminal. all of it. if it doesn't work for those, it isn't done.

  3. 03

    inclusivity without performance.

    no rainbow logo in june and bad behavior in july. build for everyone. hire that way too.

  4. 04

    small over scalable.

    one thing beautifully. not a framework nobody asked for.

  5. 05

    open by default.

    source. standards. no lock-in.

  6. 06

    done beats perfect.

    ship the fragment. polish later. other fragments are waiting.

04 / counterweights

what we aren't.

  • a startup. no VC. no exit. no growth theater.
  • a platform. fragments don't aspire to be ecosystems.
  • a bootcamp. education is a side effect of making real things.
  • solemn. dorky, yes. self-important, no.
05 / team

one, for now.

one person. designed to work at one. keeps working as more show up.

each contributor brings a fragment. the studio is the sum. people bring what they're good at and make fragments of their own — frgmt gets bigger the way a mosaic does: piece by piece, not by dilution.
